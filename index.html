~A New Intentional Living Community~

the vision

Built in 1889 and named after the house’s builder and original owner, William Wilkins de Winton, the de Winton Manor is an intentional living community. The vision for the de Winton Manor is of a collaborative community of respectful, contemplative, easy going people who like to share:

laughs meals friends habits lifestyles
snuggles conversations books ideas etc.

the house

The house, located at 24th St. and Dolores St. in the Mission, has tall ceilings, hardwood floors, shared front room living and dining space, and a sizeable kitchen. Off the kitchen is a back porch overlooking a backyard. Upstairs there are 4 bedrooms. On the lower level is a one bedroom in-law unit.

the need

The de Winton Manor is looking for occupants for 3 of the upstairs bedrooms, 2 of which are large enough to be shared. We are looking for people who are kind, tidy, productive, social, generous, etc. We want to host weekly community potluck dinners, open to all. As a group, we should decide how often we want to host these.

the application

Applications can feel impersonal so instead we ask that you make a short (no more than 5 min) video of yourself talking about why you want to live in the de Winton Manor. Don’t spend a lot of time on this! The more awkward the better! Please also include links to things you’ve put on the internet and/or things you like on the internet. Email your application to dewintonmanor@gmail.com.
