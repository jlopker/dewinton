the vision

Built in 1889 and named after the house’s builder and original owner, William Wilkins de Winton, the de Winton Manor is an intentional living community. The vision for the de Winton Manor is of a collaborative community of respectful, contemplative, easy going people who like to share:

laughs food meals friends habits lifestyles
snuggles conversations books ideas

the house

The house, located on the Mission-Noe Valley border, has tall ceilings, hardwood floors, shared front room living and dining space, and a sizeable kitchen. Off the kitchen is a back porch overlooking a backyard. Upstairs there are 4 bedrooms. On the lower level is a one bedroom in-law unit.

kind tidy social generous
